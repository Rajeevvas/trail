Type "Control Panel" and click on the matching search result to open the Control Panel.
In the Control Panel, change the "View by" option to "Large icons" or "Small icons" to make it easier to find the necessary settings.
Locate and click on the "User Accounts" or "User Accounts and Family Safety" option. The exact name may vary depending on the version of Windows you're using.
Look for the "Manage User Accounts" or "Manage another account" option and click on it.
You should see a list of user accounts on your laptop. Select the user account that you want to rename.
Click on the "Properties" or "Change the account name" option.
Enter the new name you want to assign to the user account and click on "OK" or "Change Name" to save the changes.
